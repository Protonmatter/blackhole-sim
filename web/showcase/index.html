Guided session

1. Spin / photon ring

Increase spin. The shadow and inner emission become asymmetric because frame dragging changes ray trajectories and disk orbital speeds.

2. Inclination / Doppler beaming

Move toward edge-on. The approaching side brightens and the receding side dims.

3. Stokes / Faraday terms

Raise Faraday rotation or conversion. The diagnostic polarization overlay changes even when total intensity is similar.

4. Progressive full-HD path

Use profiles to mimic Surface or workstation render budgets. This shows why tiles and progressive passes are required.
